Privacy Policy - Uniride

Last Updated: 20-08-2025

Welcome to Uniride! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the information you provide when using our application. By using Uniride, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Information you provide directly: Full name, phone number, email address, profile photo, driver’s license (drivers only), vehicle details and photos (make, model, license plate, year).
  • Information collected automatically: Device information (type, OS), IP address, unique device identifiers, geolocation data, usage data (login time, features accessed, trip history).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and improve our services.
  • Verify drivers’ identities and ensure reliability.
  • Match riders with nearby drivers.
  • Provide customer support and resolve issues.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, or trip details (with your consent).
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may only be shared with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, payment, mapping, analytics).
  • Legal authorities if required by law or court order.
  • Other users (minimal data such as driver name, car details, and phone number during a trip).

4. Data Security

We apply strong security measures to protect your information. However, no online data transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Request limitation of data sharing with third parties.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Major changes will be communicated through the app or email.
